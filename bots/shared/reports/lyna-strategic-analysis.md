# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

## The $12 Billion Problem

American hospitals face a silent crisis that drains $12 billion annually: **nurses lose an average of 45 minutes per shift navigating outdated communication systems**. This inefficiency manifests through paging through operators, walking to phones, logging into computers, and waiting for callbacks.

**Critical Impact:**
- 70% of serious safety events are linked to communication failures
- Nurses leave critical patients unattended to make calls
- Sterile fields broken in isolation rooms
- 15+ minutes wasted per urgent communication

## LYNA: The Solution

LYNA is a **voice-activated wearable smart badge** designed specifically for nursing clinical workflow, featuring:

| Capability | Current State | With LYNA |
|------------|---------------|-----------|
| Voice Communication | 3-5 minutes via operator | 5 seconds direct |
| Clinical Decision Support | Leave bedside, log in | Voice query at point of care |
| Care Coordination | Multiple systems | Intelligent routing |

**Key Features:**
- Hands-free badge clipping to scrubs
- 12-hour snap-and-swap battery
- Barcode scanner and RFID room detection
- Local-first AI (SLM on hospital premises)
- Native Epic/Cerner integration
- Multi-language translation

## Strategic Recommendation

**Pursue aggressive but disciplined market entry targeting 50 pilot hospitals within 18 months**, prioritizing large regional health systems where ROI is most compelling. The market window is 18-24 months before well-funded competitors recognize the opportunity.
